Mechanisms of Perception in the Human Brain

You are invited to help us teach robots how to hear!

Dr. Matthew Tata and his research lab would like to record your voice speaking a few simple phrases, each a few seconds long. The recordings will be used to train neural networks that simulate the hearing mechanisms of the human brain. The recorded audio will be stored in protected audio files that will not have any name attached to them, so your voice will be anonymous.

This should only take you a few minutes, but you can stop before the end if you want to. For more information about our research go to tatalab.ca
